Key Scientific Findings

  • Size-dependent transfer: Smaller NPs cross more readily. Bakhamis 2025: clear 200nm cutoff for PEGylated melanin NPs. Grafmueller 2015: 3% at 78nm vs 0.5% at 499nm (PS).
  • Coating effect: PEGylation reduces translocation (55% uncoated vs 15% PEGylated at 50nm). NH2 completely blocks PS transfer. BPEI-Ag most cytotoxic (IC50=4.3 mg/L).
  • Model concordance: In vitro overestimates translocation vs ex vivo. Aengenheister: PEG-Au 0.6% in co-culture vs 0.01% in perfusion (60x difference).
  • Glucose-GNP best candidate: P:F ratio 360 with x23 yolk sac accumulation = optimal placenta-selective delivery for Shiri's research.
  • Chronic TiO2 risk: Li 2023: dose-dependent fetal Ti accumulation + EndMT-mediated placental barrier disruption at 50-250 mg/kg oral.
